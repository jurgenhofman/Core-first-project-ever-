Fast as Lightning

For all the muscle and fitness nuts among us Usain Bolt must be an inspiration or motivational factor at least. The fastest man to ever walk this planet holds the world record on the 100 and 200 meter and it does not seem that anyone will take that from him in a while.

In 2016 Bolt participated in his final Olympics. But although we may never see him perform on an Olympic field we will always remember his performances.

We want to know what it takes to become the fastest man in the world, so let's see how Bolt prepares for his big games!
