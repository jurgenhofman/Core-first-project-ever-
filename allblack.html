The All Black Workout

Some of us know the All Blacks as the only Three Time World Champion in the history of Rugby Union. But most of us will know the All Blacks because of their vicious Haka, which they perform before the start of every game:

But as vicious and intimidating as the Haka may be, it is not what we focus on. We focus on fitness, and the All Black team is the embodiment of Fitness.

So what is the secret to becoming as powerfull, agile and quick as for example All Black legend Ma'a Nonu?

For those of you who do not know: The unstoppable force by the name of Ma'a Nonu is 6ft (1.82m) and 236lbs (107 kg) and runs that 45 meters in 6 seconds! (the last few meters with and Australian defender on his back).

So, these numbers speak for itself, now check out how to become a rugby monster yourself and don't forget the featured exercises!

Oh yeah... And don't forget those abs!
