Tavi Castro Motivation

Tavi Castro is one of the worlds most famous Fitness Models but he definitely did not start that way. Your probably already know his story and his name so we are not going to pay too much attention to that in this article.

Now that Tavi has reached the top in the Fitness world he pays his attention to other fields, like vlogging, clothing and music.
In his video series called "We are the engineered" he vlogs about his travels and other endeavoures. Episode 00 is without a doubt the most inspirational video he has made.

Tavi tells his story and shows you how his hard work has led to living the life everybody dreams of. May this video motivate you and inspire you to reach your own goals!
