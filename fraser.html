The Fittests man on Earth

Matt Fraser is the 2014 an 2015 Crossfit games runner up and the 2016 winner and he is without a doubt the fittest man on earth.

Crossfit is perhaps the best fitness regimen in the world to really get fit. It focusses on the development of your body on basically all aspects. By combining elements of HIIT, Olympic weightlifting, plyometrics, powerlifting, gymnastics and calisthenics (to name a few) and incorporating them in functional movements this fitness regimen will accomplish a true fit body.

Matt Fraser is the true CrossFit king of the world at this moment, winning the offical 2016 CrossFit games. The CrossFit games consist of various different excersises which the contestants get familiair with only a couple of hours before the start. This means that each contestant has to be ready and prepared for anything. This element of surprise also means that contestants cannot prepare themselves for the exercises like for example weightlifters can.
