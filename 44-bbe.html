44 Best Bodyweight Exercises

Who is Tee Major? Well, fortunately he shares his bio with us!

Tee Major is a certified fitness instructor and owner of his own company called Tee Major Fitness. Oh by the way, he is also the US Military Fitness Instructor. Not bad right?
But Tee did not start his career as a succesfull entrepreneur and fitness instructor. In High School he was involved in almost every sport you can imagine, from field tracking to football and basketball.

After graduating from Bryant University he worked for the company Cintas, as a salesman for three years.

This job did not fulfull his dream of being involved in health and fitness and so Tee started his own company. Now Tee's life is all about fitness and health, as he has not only his own company, but also modelled for brands like Reebok, Adidas, Nike and Puma and became the Fitness Intructor for the US Military.

As you'll see, Tee is a master of Bodyweight training and to inspire you he shares his 44 best bodyweight exercises, check it out below!
